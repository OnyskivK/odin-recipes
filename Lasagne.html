Homemade Lasagna

Is a classic recipe that every cook should have in their rotation

~Lasagna Ingredients~

  • Cheese Filling:For this classic lasagna recipe, the filling contains
    ricotta and parmesan with seasonings, parsley
  • Meat:I use both Italian sausage and ground beef for great flavor.
    If using all beef, add a ¼ teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • Sauce:To keep this sauce quick, I use pasta sauce or marinara sauce.
    You can also stew chopped tomatoes
  • Spinach (variation):To make a spinach lasagna, squeeze out most
    of the moisture of defrosted frozen spinach and add it with the cheese layer

~Steps How to Make Lasagna~

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat brown the ground beef and
    sausage together. Drain off and discard any fat
  2. Add the minced garlic and spaghetti sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the ricotta, 2 cups of the mozzarella cheese, eggs,
    ½ cup of grated Parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt and ground black pepper
    Mix well
  4. SSpread ¾ cup of sauce on the bottom of the baking pan
  5. Cover the bottom with uncooked lasagna noodles
  6. Top with ⅓ of the cheese mixture and then ¼ of the sauce
  7. Repeat layers (noodles, cheese, sauce, noodles, cheese, sauce)
